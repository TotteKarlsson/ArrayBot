Detailed Description

The JoyStickMessageDispatcher is tailor made for the ArrayBot.

The dispatcher uses the right side of a PS3 joystick for the right side of the ArrayBot, and the left side designated for the left side of the ArrayBot. The POV buttons are used for x-y motions for the left (whisker) side of the Arraybot.
